Christmas Hero

In this project a Christmas themed game was developed during six weeks.

In the game, Christmas is behind the corner but the Christmas Spirit has disappeared due to the Cactus Boss. The gamer has to take back the Christmas Spirit into town by finding gifts and solve the riddles, and from them deliver the gifts to the right character. For every delivered gift, the Christmas Spirit will progressively grow in terms of music, decorations, and snow.

I had the responsibility of the game world design and usability testings. I also composed the background music. The game was made in Unity and the composition was made in MuseScore.

Konsolofon

Together with a collehue, this instrument was developed. It is called konsolofon, as it is made with shelf brackets ("konsoler" in Swedish) and is based on the xylophone, but is more similar in frequency to bells. The shelf brackets were cut with a notch with a specific length to achieve the aimed frequency and tone of each shelf bracket. The konsolofon is tuned in E minor blues scale.
